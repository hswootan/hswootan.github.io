Googling port 79. Shows that Finger is an application that allows you to look up information on computer users. The login name, full name, and sometimes other facts about the user you’re fingering are frequently displayed. These details could include the user’s plan and project files, login time, idle time, time mail was last read, and the user’s workplace location and phone number (if known).
